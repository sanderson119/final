Who is this woman?

Her multifaceted and eclectic style has Susan engrossed in many passion projects, with this particular website dedicated to all things design, being one of them. She is currently a Computer Science student at Great Bay Community College which is located in the seacoast region of southern New Hampshire in Portsmouth. While she makes the seque into her second year with a focus on Digital Media Communications, she has already put together a collection of imagery and content that has been received with great reviews. Susan's passions related to digital media are, video production/editing, web site development, copy writing, photoshop creations and vlogging. The production of videos being amongst a favorite of Susan, it's not supprising one may refer to her as a "Ham". You can call her ham if you'd like to but please know, Susan hasnt eaten meat in 9 months so the judgement may bring up some controversy if you try.

Being a part of that movement has been enriching to the soul.
